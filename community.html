community

the people

The community is the beating heart of our support group, they are at the centre of all our endeavour's. We have a large network of members all across London. We have operations in North West, West, South and the south east of London. Our member's are both young and old, from many different countries. They take part in such a broad range of art from drawing and painting to pottery and ceramics to music, dance, steelpan, to street art. As new members join they continue to stretch our range of art facilities and capacity. The range of arts we now provide is so diverse it really reflects the diverse nature of the group. We have a number of very unusual and unique forms of art and music brought by our members from all over the world: Chime Bells - The Chimes of Zeng Hou Yi from China, the Russian bassoon and the Zuma from central Eurasia just to name a few. Our members are so engaged in this community that they formed their own committee for organising events and activities in addition to ones we organise. WE are so proud to have such great members and pillars of the community.

Diversity

multi culturalism

London is the most ethnically diverse region in the UK, over 200 languages are spoken in the capital. We are proud to live in such a diverse city and appreciate the many benefits of living among such a large variation of people. We welcome every single individual from any background and try to reflect the same level of diversity in our staff. We are proud to say our team of staff are from ten different nations, five continents and twelve different ethnic groups.

workshops

programs

Our community led programs range form a relaxing evening of painting, music and dance, to life drawing with a professional model, we run a whole host of events and workshops. There are lots of interesting topics and media to choose from, as well as a variety of days and times. We welcome complete beginners, as well as experienced artists. We also run ad-hoc workshops for children and teens from time to time. We run special events such as wellbeing days, gallery visits and art exhibitions. Our wellbeing days are the perfect antidote to the busyness of everyday life. Join us to take some time out to unwind and explore your creative side, and once a year we run a big summer exhibition showcasing the work of our students and tutors, and raising money for worthwhile causes. Another perfect excuse to relax and enjoy being surrounded by creativity.

our premises

our facilities

We have four centre's across London. Our main site and centre of operations in Bromley is a 17,000sqft facility. Each of our centre's comprise an art studio, photography studio with a range of dark rooms and specialist equipment. There are digital darkrooms which are used for teaching digital workflows. We have print making centre's with digital, screen, mono and relief printing, plate and stone lithography, letterpress, aqua tint and etching. 3D workshops to make work in metal, ceramics, plastic and wood. We have music rooms, dance studios, film studios, and carnival art centre's.
