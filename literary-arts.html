literary arts

Our performing arts events are made up of three stages; beginner, intermediate and master. An event can last from five to seven days. We start by introducing you to the discipline with practical one-on-one personal training. The beginner level will last for 2 days, each day you will be given an online lesson to go over what you have learned on that day. On the third day, you take part in group sessions where you will continue to reinforce what you learned at the beginner stage but at an advanced level in coordination with the group. In the final stage, you will pair and work with a professional to complete the training at our master level in preparation for a solo and group performance in front of your peers. Be warned this is fast-paced and challenging but ever so rewarding. Register your interest today.

live - immersive

Additionally on completing the previous stages and completing your personal and group performance, we have teamed up with professional performing arts groups across all disciplines and TV companies to bring an immersive experience where you will be performing with a group of professionals at a well-known event live on television. You will meet up with a team professional two days before the live performance to learn and master the performance for your chosen discipline. There will then be a dress rehearsal on the day before the live performance. Some of the groups and companies we have worked with have been famous orators, writers and poets.
