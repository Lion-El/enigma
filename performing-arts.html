performing arts

Our performing art events are made up of three stages; beginner, intermediate and master. An event can last from five to seven days. We start by introducing you to the discipline with practical one to one training. The beginner level will last for 2 days, each day you will be given an online lesson to go over what you have learnt on that day. On the third day you take part in group sessions where you will continue to reinforce what you have learned at the beginner stage but at an advanced level in coordination with the group. The final stage you will pair and work with a professional to complete the training at our master level in preparation for a solo and group performance in front of your pears. Be warned this is fast paced and challenging but ever so rewarding. Register your interest today...

live - immersive

Additionally on completing the previous stages and completing your personal and group performance, we have teamed up with professional performing arts groups across all disciplines and TV companies to bring an immersive experience where you will be performing with a group of professionals at a well known event live on television. You will meet up with a team professional's two days before the live performance to learn the and master the routine for your chosen discipline. There will then be a dress rehearsal on the day before the live performance. Some of the groups and companies we have worked with have been famous musicians, Bands, Orchestra's, pop groups, hip hop artists, RnB singers and groups, film companies, stunt men and theatre companies to name a few.
